Important

Policies

Please read before booking. These policies exist to protect your experience and mine.

💳

Deposit Policy

A $15 non-refundable deposit is required to secure every appointment. This deposit is applied to your total but will not be returned under any circumstances — including no-shows, same-day cancellations, or late arrivals over 15 minutes.

🕐

Late Policy

5–10 minutes late incurs a $5 fee. Arriving 15+ minutes late will result in a cancellation and your deposit is forfeited.

💅

Nail Bed Safety

No service over foreign products, extremely thin nails, or active infections. Your nail health comes first.

🚫

No Extra Guests

No additional guests allowed during your appointment. Please reschedule if you are feeling unwell.

✨

Nail Etiquette

Please arrive with clean, bare nails. Arriving with product on your nails may reduce service time or require an additional soak-off fee.

💵

Payment Methods

Zelle, Cash App, or exact cash only. No exceptions. Remaining balance is due at time of service.

Got Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before booking.

Once you submit your booking request and send the $15 deposit, you'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours once the deposit is verified. Your slot is not confirmed until that email comes through — so please don't assume you're booked until then.
If your requested date or time isn't open, you'll be contacted via email to find an alternative. Your deposit will transfer to the new date — it won't be lost just because of availability.
Rescheduling is allowed with at least 48 hours notice and your deposit will transfer. Cancellations within 48 hours or same-day forfeit the deposit. No-shows forfeit the deposit and may require a larger deposit for future bookings.
Yes — your $15 deposit counts toward your service total. If your set comes to $65, you'll only owe $50 at the appointment. It's not an extra charge, just a way to secure your spot.
Arrive with clean, bare nails — no old product or polish. If you have a set on, a soak-off can be added for $10. Come with your inspo pics ready if you have them — it saves time and makes the whole process smoother.
A simple Gel Manicure takes roughly 45–60 minutes. A full Gel-X or Builder Gel set with Tier 2–3 art can take 1.5–2.5 hours. Please don't book if you're in a rush.
With proper care, Gel-X and Builder Gel sets typically last 3–5 weeks. Use cuticle oil daily, avoid picking, and don't use your nails as tools. The better you treat them, the longer they hold.
Remaining balance at your appointment is accepted via Zelle, Cash App, or exact cash only. No cards, no Venmo, no partial payments. Please come prepared.
No additional guests are allowed during appointments. Please make arrangements before arriving.
Services will not be performed over another tech's existing work, extremely thin or damaged nail beds, or active infections. If you're unsure whether your nails qualify, email before booking to save yourself the trip.
